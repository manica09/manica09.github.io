Hello, Self.

There's not much to say right now other than thank you. Thank you for sticking with me through everything, and for never ever giving up on us, no matter how hard things seemed to be. Thank you for being the only one I ever really knew. I know life didn't turn out as you hoped it to, but that's okay. The only thing that matters is that we have each other, and as long as we never lose sight of what we are, we will be okay. I hope you enjoy your Valentines Day, and whatever happens, please don't forget to smile.

Love,

Calvin Borja
