Hi crush!!!

Hello! I know we haven't really talked much in the past but I figured I'd write a letter, because, as cliche as it may be, it's the only way I can communicate my thoughts to you when I'm around you. How do I start? I guess you just make me happy everytime I see you. The way that you always seem to be joyful no matter what happens really puts a smile on my face. And that one time you joined the pageant at our school? I don't think I've seen anyone prettier in my life. Please stay this way always; it'd be a shame if you change. Anyway, I don't want to bother you with too many more words, but if you have time and are willing, could we hang out sometime? I'd love to be able to talk to you in person (or at least try lol). How about lunch tomorrow?

Love,

Calvin Borja
